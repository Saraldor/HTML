When it starts

The Who Shop started on the 1st of December 1984 in a warehouse in Wapping. As well as attending conventions and events all over the world, including the likes of San Diego Comic Con, The Who Shop has evolved and regenerated over the years into its present incarnation in Upton Park, East London. Run by wife and husband team, Alexandra and Kevan, The Who Shop, has been featured on numerous Television programmes and news articles. With the added TV & Film museum, The Who Shop has become a haven for all things Doctor Who for over the last 30 years, welcoming visitors from all over the world through their doors.
